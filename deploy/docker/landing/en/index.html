FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How is Simpulx different from a regular WhatsApp inbox?
A regular inbox just holds messages. Simpulx adds AI that actually executes the sale: replying, qualifying, following up, and handing hot leads to sales, complete with a score and summary.
Is my WhatsApp number safe?
Yes. Simpulx connects through Meta's official WhatsApp Cloud API. Your business number stays yours, and all conversations are stored on cloud infrastructure in the Indonesia region (Jakarta).
Does it work outside property?
Yes. The AI and catalogs are multi-segment: automotive, property, and retail. The microsite e-catalog is property-specific, while price catalogs (variant pickers) work for automotive and other segments.
How long does setup take?
You can be live within minutes of connecting your number. Our team helps with onboarding, AI voice, campaigns, and agent routing.
How are AI Credits counted?
1 credit is used each time the AI replies or summarizes lead data. Emoji and trivial messages never consume credits. When the quota runs out, you can top up and AI features resume.
