FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Do I need the WhatsApp Business API?
Yes. Simpulx connects to your WhatsApp Business number through the official Meta Cloud API, so your number stays compliant and verified.
Can I make and receive calls?
Yes. Simpulx supports the WhatsApp Business Calling API - agents can place outbound calls (with customer permission) and answer inbound calls directly in the browser.
How long does setup take?
Most teams are live within a day. Connect your number, invite your team, and you are ready to go.
How is my data handled?
Your data is encrypted in transit, stored securely, and never sold. See our Privacy Policy for details.
How much does it cost?
Pricing depends on your team size and message volume. Contact us and we will tailor a plan for you.
