Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Simpulx ("Simpulx", "we", "us") collects, uses, shares and protects information when you use our website at simpulx.com and the Simpulx application at app.simpulx.com (together, the "Service"). By using the Service you agree to this Policy.

Simpulx is a WhatsApp customer engagement platform used by businesses to manage conversations and calls with their own customers. For the customer data that our business clients process through Simpulx, the business is the data controller and Simpulx acts as a data processor on their behalf.

1. Information we collect

a. Account information

  • Name, email address, phone number and role of users you create.
  • Authentication data such as a securely hashed password and session tokens.
  • Organization and team settings you configure.

b. WhatsApp & conversation data

When you connect a WhatsApp Business number through the official Meta WhatsApp Business Platform (Cloud API), we process, on your behalf:

  • Messages and media exchanged between your business and your customers.
  • Customer contact details such as name and phone number (WhatsApp ID).
  • Message templates, broadcasts and automation you configure.
  • Voice call metadata, including call status and call duration (we do not record call audio).

c. Usage & technical data

  • Log data such as IP address, browser/device type, and timestamps.
  • Product usage events used to operate, secure and improve the Service.
  • Cookies and similar technologies (see Section 8).

2. How we use information

  • Provide, operate and maintain the Service (inbox, calling, broadcasts, templates, automation, analytics).
  • Route conversations and calls to the correct agent and enable WhatsApp voice calling.
  • Authenticate users and keep accounts secure.
  • Provide support, respond to requests, and send service-related notices.
  • Monitor, troubleshoot, prevent abuse, and improve performance and reliability.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell your personal data, and we do not use the contents of your customers' WhatsApp messages for advertising.

3. WhatsApp Business Platform

The Service integrates with the WhatsApp Business Platform operated by Meta. Your use of WhatsApp through Simpulx is also subject to Meta's and WhatsApp's terms and policies. Data sent to or received from WhatsApp is processed by Meta in accordance with Meta's terms. We only request the WhatsApp permissions necessary to provide the Service (messaging and, where enabled, calling), and we access your WhatsApp data solely to operate the Service for you.

4. Google Analytics & Google Ads

When you connect a Google account to view reporting in Simpulx (for example, Google Analytics 4 or Google Ads), we request only the access needed for that feature, using scopes such as analytics.readonly and adwords. We use this access solely to read and display the analytics and advertising metrics for the property or account you choose to connect, inside your Simpulx reports.

Simpulx's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. Specifically, we do not:

  • use Google user data for advertising, or transfer it to data brokers;
  • sell Google user data;
  • use it for any purpose other than providing or improving the user-facing features you connected it for;
  • allow humans to read it, except with your consent, for security or to comply with law, or when the data is aggregated and anonymized.

You can disconnect a Google account at any time from Simpulx (Settings → Channel & Integrations), or revoke access from your Google account permissions. On disconnect, we stop accessing the data and delete the stored authorization token.

5. How we share information

We share information only as needed to run the Service:

  • Service providers (sub-processors) who help us operate, such as cloud hosting and infrastructure (e.g. Amazon Web Services) and the WhatsApp Business Platform (Meta).
  • Legal and safety: when required by law, regulation, legal process, or to protect the rights, property or safety of Simpulx, our users, or the public.
  • Business transfers: in connection with a merger, acquisition or sale of assets, with notice where required.

6. Data retention

We retain personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service, and thereafter as required to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes and enforce agreements. Business customers may request export or deletion of the data they control, subject to legal limits.

7. Security

We protect data with technical and organizational measures, including encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS), access controls, hashed credentials, and restricted, role-based access. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to protect your information and continuously improve our safeguards.

8. Cookies

We use cookies and local storage that are necessary to operate the Service, such as keeping you signed in and remembering preferences. You can control cookies through your browser settings; disabling essential cookies may affect how the Service works.

9. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data, and to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact us at admin@simpulx.com. If your data is managed by a business using Simpulx, please contact that business; we will assist them as their processor.

10. International transfers

Your information may be processed and stored in countries other than your own, including where our service providers operate. Where required, we put appropriate safeguards in place for such transfers.

11. Children's privacy

The Service is intended for businesses and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us personal data, contact us and we will delete it.

12. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version here and revise the "Last updated" date above. Material changes may be communicated through the Service.
